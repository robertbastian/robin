Welcome to Robin.

Robin allows friends, colleagues, or even mortal enemies to challenge each other.

A round-robin tournament is set up, and each participant can add their own challenges. After the deadline for each challenge, the setter compares the solutions and gives each a rating, but moreover, each participant rates the challenge that was set.

Thus Robin improves your programming not only by encouraging you to write good answers, but to write good questions as well.

Join the party.

Start challenging others and challenge yourself. Discover the beauty of Robin.
